About Burr's Painting LLC

Industrial Surface Preparation & Protective Coating Specialists

Burr's Painting LLC protects public and industrial assets from humidity, water submersion, chemical exposure, and corrosion. Our crews support water management infrastructure, civil works, municipal facilities, pump stations, piping systems, and industrial flooring projects.

Located in Okeechobee, Florida, we are positioned for rapid mobilization across South Florida with crews, equipment, and specialty coating capabilities.

Core Capabilities

  • Industrial surface preparation and abrasive blasting
  • High-performance epoxy and urethane coatings
  • NSF coatings for municipal pumps and piping
  • Polyurethane injection and leak mitigation
  • Epoxy and polyaspartic floor coating systems
